I built this website for the tribute of those singers/songwriters I am a big fan of. Treat this as my personal blog, diary, or whatever you can think of!

Ed Sheeran

Ed Sheeran is my most favorite singer/songwriter. His music is a great inspiration to me in my pursuit of art. His melody and lyrics truly touch the soul of the audiences around the world.

米津玄師

He was the reason of bringing me to J-Pop: The talented singer, producer, illustrator, and dancer. However, Kenshi's music is indeed an unique dish on the table of melody in "The Land of The Raising Sun". An attractive voice and mysterious style are the difference of this artist.

Den

If you are fluent in Vietnamese, I'm sure you will be intoxicated by his melody. Even though a rapper, the music that Den pursues has a soft tone and a desire to seek peace and tranquility, away from the noise of life flow. A talented singer in my country: a master of rhytming and a writer of great stories

Passengers

He is the writer of the hit "Let Her Go", but never call him as "One Hit Wonder". His miracle guitar and his unique voice will bring us to the sad, soft and poetic songs. Once you listen to Passergers, you'll never escape. He and Ed Sheeran are my most favorite US - UK artists of all time. Their collab - "Hearts' On Fire" - was a masterpiece.

BIGBANG

The only band/artists I love in South Korean. Their music is great after all, no word can describe. Over ten years and they have been the pride of the Korean: Legends.
